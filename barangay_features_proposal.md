# 💡 Baesys — Barangay System Future Features Proposal

To elevate the Barangay Baesa Management System to a comprehensive, citizen-centric digital ecosystem, we propose several impactful features. These focus primarily on **resident empowerment, convenience, and real-time interaction**, while optimizing staff administration.

---

## 📱 Resident-Facing Features

### 1. 💳 Online Payments Integration
* **Concept**: Allow residents to pay for document requests (e.g., Barangay Clearance, Business Permits) directly through the portal before pick-up.
* **Technology**: Integrate a payment gateway like **PayMongo**, **GCash API**, or **Maya** to accept mobile e-wallets, credit cards, or over-the-counter payments.
* **Benefits**:
  - Eliminates physical Cashier queues.
  - Ensures correct fee collection before printing documents.
  - Generates secure, instant digital receipts.

### 2. 🚨 Community Emergency Alerts & SOS System
* **Concept**: A real-time panic/SOS button in the resident portal app interface for reporting immediate local emergencies (fire, medical, security) directly to the Barangay Command Center.
* **Features**:
  - One-tap emergency broadcast with real-time location mapping.
  - Admin dashboard displaying active SOS alerts with resident details and location.
  - SMS notification broadcast to nearby Purok leaders or emergency services.
  - Safety status check-ins during typhoons/natural disasters.

### 3. 🩺 Health Clinic & Facility Appointment Scheduling
* **Concept**: A booking portal for barangay services, including the Barangay Health Center, dental checks, free vaccinations, or booking barangay multi-purpose courts.
* **Features**:
  - Live calendar showing available slots, service types, and attending health officers.
  - Automated SMS/Email confirmations and reminder alerts.
  - Vaccine card/health records storage in the resident's profile.

### 4. 🪪 Digital Barangay ID & Mobile Pass
* **Concept**: A secure digital ID card directly accessible from the resident portal profile.
* **Features**:
  - QR Code containing verified resident ID and household details.
  - Fast scan verification for relief distribution, community events, and aid entry checkpoints.
  - Printable PDF format with cryptographic signature.

### 5. 📢 Resident Incident Reporting & Feedback Portal
* **Concept**: An interactive citizen reporting dashboard where residents can report non-emergency public concerns (e.g., street light out, trash accumulation, stray animals, flooded street).
* **Features**:
  - Photo upload and description of the concern.
  - Tracking system (Status: *Reported ➔ Assigned ➔ In Progress ➔ Resolved*).
  - Upvoting system for issues affecting multiple neighbors (Purok level).

### 6. 🏠 Relief Distribution & Social Services Tracker
* **Concept**: Digital tracking for community programs (relief goods, senior citizen pensions, student scholarships, single-parent aid).
* **Features**:
  - Automated notifications when a resident is eligible for a specific distribution program.
  - Barcode scanning on delivery to prevent double-claiming and ensure complete transparency.

---

## ⚙️ Admin & Staff-Facing Enhancements

### 1. 📊 Barangay Demographic Analytics Dashboard
* **Concept**: Visual data dashboards demonstrating real-time statistics (age groups, employment status, population density per Purok, gender distribution).
* **Benefits**: Helps local officials draft policies, allocate budget resources effectively, and easily prepare reports for the city government.

### 2. 🗓️ Interactive Hearing & Mediation Scheduler (Blotter cases)
* **Concept**: Integrate calendar scheduling directly into the Blotter Registry to book case hearings, automatically notifying all parties (complainants, respondents, and mediators) via SMS.

### 3. 📂 Digital Document Archiving with OCR
* **Concept**: Scan old paper-based barangay records and use Optical Character Recognition (OCR) to convert them into searchable digital files, archiving them securely.
